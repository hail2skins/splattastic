Our mission is to bring the community of springboard and platform diving together.

The diving team is often smaller and apart from the swim team as many have to practice and compete in different locations. Though it's a team sport, it is intensely individualistic. This site will give divers a community to share successes and splats and build friendships through this amazing sport.
